My UX Skills

User Research

Part of my user research involves coming up with interview questions, interviewing participants, creating a proto persona and doing a competitor analysis.

User Empathy

When I try to empathize with my users, I make an empathy map, I do ideation, and I make a priority matrix about what would be important for them and/also the company.

User Testing

I draw and sketch wireframe ideas on pencil and paper. I take my ideas and put them onto Adobe XD or Figma to create a low fidelity prototype. I gather users to try out my prototype to find any pain points or any insights.
